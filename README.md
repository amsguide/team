<p>This Privacy Policy explains how the AMS mobile application ("the App") handles user information.</p>

<h2>1. General Information</h2>
<p>
The AMS application is a clinical decision-support tool intended for healthcare professionals.
The app provides information related to antimicrobial therapy, dosing recommendations, and
clinical calculators.
</p>

<p>
The information provided in the app is for educational and decision-support purposes only and
does not replace professional medical judgement.
</p>

<h2>2. Personal Data</h2>
<p>
The application does not collect, store, or process personal data of users.
</p>

<p>
The app does not require user registration and does not request personal identifiers such as
name, email address, phone number, or location.
</p>

<h2>3. Patient Data</h2>
<p>
The application does not store patient data.
Any clinical values entered by the user (such as laboratory values, weight, or renal function)
are processed locally on the device and are not transmitted to external servers.
</p>

<h2>4. Analytics</h2>
<p>
The application may use anonymous analytics services (such as Firebase Analytics) to collect
aggregated information about how the app is used. These data help improve the performance,
stability, and usability of the application.
</p>

<p>
No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.
</p>

<h2>5. Third-Party Services</h2>
<p>
The application may use third-party services for technical purposes such as analytics
or crash reporting. These services may collect anonymous usage information according
to their own privacy policies.
</p>

<h2>6. Data Security</h2>
<p>
Because the application does not store personal or patient data on external servers,
no sensitive user information is retained by the developer.
</p>

<h2>7. Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. When changes are made,
